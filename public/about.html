About Freddy

Freddy's Barbershop is more than just a cut — it's a community. With years of experience and a dedication to precision, Freddy brings personality and pride to every trim, fade, and shave.

Located in the heart of the city, Freddy’s is known for its old-school vibe, modern techniques, and welcoming energy. When you sit in Freddy’s chair, you’re more than a customer — you’re family.
